About Us

We are an organisation that supports coffee farmers all over the world. We ask the farmers to roast their beans themselves after the harvest and then put them in reusable jute bags (offered by us).

As a result, we reduce the work that still needs to be done in our country so we pay farmers what they work for.

We give the farmers 70% of the price we pay here for coffee beans. 20% of the price goes to the transportation of the beans. The remaining 10% go to the organisation in order to expand.

Origin

The name "Kafa" originates from the place where coffee beans were first found. Kaffa means coffee in Ethiopia.
